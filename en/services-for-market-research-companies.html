Services for market research companies

Home › Services for market research companies

MysteryClient offers its services to several companies specializing in market research, to which we can provide the Mystery shopping projects complete with:

  • Selection and training of resources
  • Validation, proof reading and translations of results
  • Report in raw data to be processed
  • Finished report processing

We can provide reporting data through customized versions of our platform accessible through dedicated links (www.nomeazienda.it), and with the logos of the company reselling the service. All information that can be acquired from reports and web panel is linked to the company itself.

In this way, those who do not specialize in activities with the mystery customer can enlarge their bouquet of services by adding this methodology, which, as opposed to normal market research, assumes a large turnover of resources employed in verification.

Services can be purchased for resale to its customers by:

  • Market research company
  • Personnel selection and training company
  • Quality certification bodies
  • Inspection bodies.

Mystery shopping can be used to give value to one's products by monitoring one's suppliers by outsourcing the checks to a third-party body such as ours; here are some possible examples:

  • Advertising and leaflet distribution company
  • Hostess and promoter company
  • Display and window dressing company.

We make our equipped spaces available to companies and market research institutes for focus groups, product tests, usability tests, meetings, individual and group interviews, and refresher and training courses.

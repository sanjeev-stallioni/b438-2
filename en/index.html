The Mystery Client, what is it

The Mystery Client is an indispensable tool for companies that need to assess and measure customer satisfaction, the application of company standards, quality of services, employee behavior, procedures, and merchandising; and it does so incognito, through the mystery shopper figure. The Mystery Client is used in all types of companies: from large-scale retail, to BANKS, to CALL CENTERS, to CLINICS to HOTELS
